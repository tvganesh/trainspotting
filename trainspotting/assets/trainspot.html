Trainspotting

Never be late for a train or too early at the station! Locate your favorite trains  at any moment.

Favorites: Maintain a list of your favorite trains. You can 'add', 'delete' or 'deleteAll' trains by selecting from the Options Menu.

Locate: Locate any train by selecting one of your favorite train from the drop down list. Choose an appropriate day and then choose 'Locate Train'.
